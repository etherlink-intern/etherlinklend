## Scope

In scope:

- Etherlink Morpho deployment configuration.
- Market configuration templates.
- Oracle integration assumptions.
- Deployment and verification procedures.
- Documentation that could cause unsafe deployment or user behavior.

Unmodified upstream submodules SHOULD be reported upstream unless the issue arises from this integration or deployment process.

## Out Of Scope

- Social engineering.
- Spam or denial-of-service against public infrastructure.
- Attacks requiring physical access.
- Issues in unrelated third-party services.
- Claims based only on scanner output without exploitability explanation.

## Severity Levels

- Critical: live fund loss or immediate exploitability.
- High: serious vulnerability with plausible fund-loss path.
- Medium: limited impact or special conditions required.
- Low: non-critical correctness, process, or documentation issue.
- Informational: useful hardening or clarity improvement.
